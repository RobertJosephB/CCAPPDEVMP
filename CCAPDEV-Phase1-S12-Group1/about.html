About the Company/ Our Story


EZPC is a computer retail company that was built on the principles of simplicity,
usability, and outstanding customer service. However, this local technology
retail pioneer arose from modest beginnings when three friends with a
similar love for technology started tracking down secondhand PCs in the Facebook Marketplace
br>online community to resell at reasonable prices.

EZ PC officially got its name when it opened its first store in Manila in September 2020. A play
on the expression ez which in games is slang for easy, the name was a key step in establishing a brand that promised
reliable services and quality products. Eventually, the business shifted to selling brand new
products at accessible prices.

Today, as EZPC operates seven branches strong, our dedication to growth also extends to each and
every customer. We strive to provide solutions, reliable services, and affordable but quality
technologies that will enhance the lives of our customers and community. As we move to greater
heights, providing the best customer experience is our number one goal.

Mission


EZPC is committed to enhancing the technological knowledge and experience of every Filipino by
providing reliable, high-quality products and excellent customer service.

Vision


EZPC's vision is to uplift the Filipino community by creating an exceptional customer experience.
We strive to become the go-to for anyone who wants access to technology – whether these are
High-end products or those that cater to normal day-to-day needs.
